The Method

Comedy is not subjective.  It is objective - it follows a set of rules that can take years to learn on your own, or a fraction of that time when someone shows them to you. We have broken it down to three steps that you will learn in this specific order:

First we show you the rules. Gain an understanding of how it all works - the parts of a joke and their functions, why people laugh, how to connect, and much more.  

Second, you learn the method used by the top comedians to write material that is much faster, more effective, and completely different than what new comedians do.  

Lastly you will learn the specific  techniques used for years by professional comedians to win over audiences, deal with hecklers, create excitement, and much much more
